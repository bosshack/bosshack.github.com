First BOSS Hack-A-Thon Scheduled

Nov 27th, 2012 | Comments

Birmingham Open Source Software has scheduled our first Hack-A-Thon for 26-Jan-2013, also known as the last Saturday in January, 2013.

Several potential projects were suggested at our last BOSS meetup. This list will most likely grow, so make sure you check back often. Additional nominations are encouraged - just post your suggestions in the comments

We’re planning on having a virtual meeting in December to nail down the details. Date and time of the December meeting will be posted on the BOSS Meetup Page
