BOSS Hack Planning Meetup

Dec 11th, 2012 | Comments

Date/Time 6pm 17-Dec-2012
Location Google Hangout or isotope | eleven Offices
Signup Meetup

BOSS will hold it’s December meetup at 6pm CST on 17-Dec, 2012. Since the holidays are so near and many of our regular attendees have a busy social calendar this meeting will be via Google Hangout. Josh has scheduled the hangout so click the link to get a reminder.

Participation in the hangout is optional. Members that prefer to attend the meeting at the isotope | eleven Offices are encoraged to do so.

Our adgenda for this meeting will be:

  • Planning the logistics for the Hack-A-Thon
  • Reviewing the current list of project nominations
  • Whatever else comes up…

As is the case with any hacker meeting, familiarity with the technology involved (Google Hangout) is encouraged. You may desire to share your screen, mute your audio, or put a mustache on your image. Be Prepared….

Hope to see you on Monday!
